# The Matrix

The Matrix combines Hong Kong action cinema with cyberpunk sci-fi to create a unique and influential film.

## Red Pill or Blue Pill?

Neo (Keanu Reeves) discovers the truth about his reality and joins a rebellion against the machines.

## Impact

The "bullet time" effect and the philosophical questions raised by the film continue to resonate today.
